Why AI-Resistant Design Matters

Redesigning assessments isn't just about preventing cheating—it's about fundamentally improving teaching and learning.

Preserving Academic Integrity

When students can easily outsource work to AI, we lose our ability to accurately measure what they've actually learned.

The Goal: Not to catch cheaters, but to make authentic work more valuable and compelling than AI-generated shortcuts.


AI-Resistant Design Ensures:

  • We assess genuine student understanding, not AI capabilities
  • Grades reflect actual skill development
  • Students are accountable for their own learning
  • Academic credentials maintain their meaning and value

Closing Learning Outcome Gaps

The process of completing assignments is where deep learning happens. When AI does the work, students miss out.

The Reality: AI-resistant assignments ensure students engage in the cognitive processes that build lasting competence.


What is lost when AI does the work:

  • Critical thinking development through wrestling with complex problems
  • Knowledge construction through research and synthesis
  • Skill building through practice and iteration
  • Metacognitive awareness of their own learning process

Preparing for Real-World Complexity

The most important work in any field involves problems that AI alone cannot solve.

The Future: By designing for authentic capabilities, we prepare students for work that needs human judgment.


Authentically Human Capabilities:

  • Navigating ambiguous situations with multiple stakeholders
  • Making ethical decisions with incomplete information
  • Integrating specialized domain knowledge with local context
  • Building relationships and communicating with specific audiences
  • Adapting general solutions to unique constraints
