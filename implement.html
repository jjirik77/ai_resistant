2. Assignment Redesigns (40% of Grade)

The AIAS Framework

Ensure your redesigns hit at least one of these pillars:

  • Authentic: Connects to real-world contexts or personal experience.
  • Iterative: Requires drafts, process work, or reflection.
  • Applied: Requires application of specific concepts.
  • Singular: Unique to the specific class moment/context.
