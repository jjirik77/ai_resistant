Local Policy Impact Analysis

Target: Social Science Students
Instructions: In this module, you will analyze the real-world impact of municipal decisions.
  1. Select a policy from the city council or school board enacted in the past 12 months. Reason: Recent & Hyper-Local Data
  2. Interview 3-5 affected community members face-to-face. Reason: Physical Presence Required
  3. Attend one local government meeting and document the proceedings. Reason: Verifiable Physical Action
  4. Apply course theories (Stakeholder Analysis, Equity Framework) to your specific findings. Reason: Unique Synthesis of Data
Audit Complete: High Resistance This assignment rates High. It relies on data that doesn't exist on the internet yet (recent local meetings) and requires physical human interaction.

Authentic

Data must be local and first-hand. If the answer is on Google, ChatGPT has it. Force students to become the source of new information.

Iterative

Focus on process. Require proposals, transcripts, and drafts. AI is great at final products, bad at showing the messy work of learning.

Applied

Don't ask "What is Equity?" Ask "How does the Equity Framework apply to the parking ticket data you collected yesterday?"

Singular

Encourage unique combinations. A student analyzing this library with these 3 interviewees creates a dataset of one.
