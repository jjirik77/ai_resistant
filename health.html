The "Textbook Case"

Status: Highly Vulnerable
NURS 301: Patient Safety & Quality Spring 2025
Midterm Essay: Error Theory

Instructions: Write a 4-page paper explaining the "Swiss Cheese Model" of accident causation.

Apply this theory to the "Medication Administration Error at General Hospital" case study found in Chapter 4 of your textbook. Discuss where the system failed.

  1. Explain "Swiss Cheese Model".
  2. Apply to Chapter 4 Textbook Case.
  3. Write a 4-page standard essay.
Vulnerability Report
  • Training Data Contamination: The textbook case study (Chapter 4) is likely in the training data of major LLMs. AI knows the "correct" answer already.
  • Text-Only Output: The essay format allows AI to generate a fluent, grammatically perfect response in seconds without critical thinking.
  • Abstract Application: Applying a theory to a static text description does not require the messy, real-time judgment calls of clinical practice.

The Clinical Shield

In Healthcare education, we defend against AI by requiring students to engage with real, protected clinical environments and data.

1. Authentic Data

Use internal, HIPAA-protected records or real-time clinical events. AI cannot access the specific patient file from your ward yesterday.

2. Human Process

Require interaction with staff. Interviews, shift handoffs, and huddles create verbal data that AI cannot forge.

3. Clinical Application

Don't ask for definitions. Ask "How did the safety protocol fail in this specific incident on 3-West?"

4. Singular Context

Every unit and shift is unique. A student analyzing this near-miss on this shift creates a dataset of one.

The "RCA Project"

Status: AI Resistant
NURS 301: Patient Safety & Quality Spring 2025
Project: Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

Instructions: Select a de-identified safety event or "near miss" from your current clinical rotation site that occurred in the past 6 months.

  • Data Collection: Interview 2 staff members involved in the workflow regarding the event.
  • Observation: Observe the physical environment where the event occurred and document layout/distractions.
  • Analysis: Submit a completed RCA diagram and a 2-page executive summary of your findings.
  1. Select a de-identified internal case (past 6 months).
  2. Interview 2 staff members involved in the workflow.
  3. Observe physical environment (layout, distractions).
Verification Successful

This assignment successfully applies the Clinical Shield strategies:

  • Authentic Data: Uses HIPAA-protected internal records that AI cannot access.
  • Process Focus: Interviews and observations require physical presence and human interaction.
  • Singular Context: The analysis is tied to a specific unit, time, and physical layout unique to the student's clinical site.
