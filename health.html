Patient Safety Incident Analysis

Target: Healthcare/Nursing Students
Instructions: In this module, you will investigate a recent patient safety incident in your clinical setting.
  1. Select a de-identified case from hospital records (past 6 months). Reason: Protected Clinical Data
  2. Interview 2-3 healthcare professionals involved in the incident. Reason: Professional Insight
  3. Observe relevant clinical processes and the physical environment. Reason: Physical Observation
  4. Apply safety frameworks (Swiss Cheese Model, RCA) to analyze the incident. Reason: Clinical Application
Audit Complete: High Resistance This assignment rates High. It relies on protected clinical data (HIPAA) and requires physical presence in the clinical setting.

Authentic

Data must be clinical and real. If the case study is generic or online, AI can solve it. Use internal, de-identified patient records.

Iterative

Focus on the investigation process. Require interview notes, root cause diagrams, and draft reports before the final analysis.

Applied

Don't ask "What is the Swiss Cheese Model?" Ask "How did the safety layers fail in this specific medication error?"

Singular

Every patient and unit is unique. A student analyzing this fall on this ward creates a dataset of one.
