The Path Forward

Why Redesign Assessments?

The Trust Deficit

When students can easily outsource work to AI, we lose confidence in what they've actually learned. The diploma signifies skill acquisition; if the skills are outsourced, the credential loses value.

The Skills Gap

If AI completes the work, students miss critical skill development opportunities. The struggle of writing and solving problems is where the actual neural pathways are formed.

Real World Readiness

Students need authentic experience with complex, contextual problems they'll face in their careersâ€”problems AI alone cannot solve. Future jobs will require managing AI, not competing with it on basic tasks.
